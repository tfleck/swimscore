Hey, it looks like it's your first time here! Here's some tips
First, select your scoring system using the dropdown that says "PIAA" at the top of your screen, it looks like this
Then, as the meet goes on, tap on the places that you team wins for each event
They will turn green, and be counted towards the "Home" score, like this
If there is a DQ for either team in a scoring place, click or tap the place again, and it will turn red. These points won't go to either team.
If you need to make any changes, keep tapping and the button will cycle through all the choices. Places left outlined like this will be counted towards the "Away" score
As you enter information for each event, the events will drop to the bottom of the table in reverse order
The other button at the top will pull up important times for your league, it looks like this
